     - **What is Automation, Scaling, and Infrastructure:**
       Scaling: Automation scalability refers to handling these varying resource requirements dynamically without manual intervention. Automation scalability helps an organization in many ways.
       Infrastructure: Infrastructure automation is the process of reducing human interaction with IT systems by creating scripts or functions that are repeatable and can be used either by other software or on command.
       Automation: Automation in DevOps is the process of designing, developing and deploying software and systems with minimal human intervention.
